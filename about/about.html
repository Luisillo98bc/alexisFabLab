About Me

Hi, my name is Alexis Del Aguila Ramos, I´m Mechatronics Engineer, I’m Peruvian, I live in Huancayo City, I work at the Fab Lab Ucontinental developing projects in technology, biomedical engineer, automation, and education. I was born in the unconquerable city of Huancayo, the only one that could not be conquered. From a very young age, I developed a fascination for building things, experimenting with the operation of toys, among other activities. Throughout my life, I have lived in different cities, learning about their customs, and living close to people's needs, which helped me develop problem-solving skills. During the course of my career, my mother had an accident in which she ended up with fractures in both hips, which propelled me to immerse myself in the world of biotechnology for the development of biomedical prosthetics, becoming my strongest specialty. I have developed many projects in different sectors such as robotics, environment, education, automation, medicine, mechanics, and electronics. I have worked on designing parts, programming, and assembling circuits. One of my most representative projects is the donation of 3D-printed hand and arm prosthetics to low-income individuals, having a significant social impact and leaving a legacy of altruism in my region.
